Facts

What is mental health? It’s an expression we use every day, so it might surprise you that the term ‘mental health’ is frequently misunderstood. ‘Mental health’ is often used as a substitute for mental health conditions – such as depression, anxiety conditions, schizophrenia, and others.

Get Support

Our online community is all about supporting and learning from each other. To reply to posts or start a new thread, you'll need to be a member. Please be aware that our forums are community-led and not staffed by mental health professionals. To talk to someone one-on-one, please contact us.
